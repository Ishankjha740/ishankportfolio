# Welcome to my Portfolio project

This website is the **professional portfolio of Ishank Jha**, a Brand Strategist and Content Architect based in Hyderabad, India.

It functions as a digital showcase of his career, expertise, and work in social media strategy, brand building, and data-driven marketing. Key sections include:

1   **Professional Identity:** It highlights his unique approach of combining creativity, data analytics, and scalable systems to help brands grow.

2   **Experience & Education:** It details his background, including an MBA in Analytics & Marketing, and his professional experience with companies like GMR Hyderabad International Airport, Cog Culture, and UNFPA.

3   **Services:** It outlines the specific services he provides, ranging from social media strategy and content direction to performance marketing and analytics-based growth systems.

4   **Portfolio & Case Studies:** It features concrete examples of his work, demonstrating how he has scaled social media reach, built content engines, and improved brand positioning for various clients.

5   **Contact & Skills:** It provides his contact information, LinkedIn/Behance profiles, and a breakdown of the technical tools and AI platforms he uses to execute his strategies.
